Keyboard Shortcuts

These Keyboard shortcuts are supported:
  • LEFT, RIGHT, UP, DOWN: Move ion the presentation.
  • ESC: Switch to between presentation overview back and forth.
  • S: Open Speaker view window.
  • E: Toggle between presentation and PDF print view.
